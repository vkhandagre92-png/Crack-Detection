Ceaser – Detecting Cracks, Ensuring Safety

Ceaser specializes in cutting-edge rail track inspection solutions utilizing acoustic wave technology. By analyzing wave propagation, we detect critical anomalies such as cracks and corrosion, ensuring the structural integrity of rail tracks. Our innovative solution offers real-time, high-accuracy monitoring, non-destructive testing, and has a sustainable environmental impact. With advanced signal processing algorithms, IoT integration, and cost-effective implementation, Ceaser provides scalable, reliable, and affordable inspection systems for safer railway operations. Ceaser is enhancing rail safety by implementing a dual-sensor system that combines acoustic emission sensors and ultrasonic sensors. This innovative approach ensures accurate crack detection and precise localization, even while trains are in motion. The acoustic emission sensors are installed on moving trains, allowing real-time detection of cracks. These sensors provide highly detailed information about the size and exact location of the defects along the rail track, ensuring early detection of potential hazards. In addition, ultrasonic sensors and receivers are placed at sensor stations along the tracks, with amplifiers positioned between stations. These sensors help determine the approximate range where cracks may occur, guiding the acoustic emission system to focus on specific areas for detailed inspection. This comprehensive system, combining the precision of acoustic emission sensors and the range-finding capability of ultrasonic sensors, ensures that rail travel is safe by detecting potential dangers well in advance, maintaining the integrity of the tracks, and preventing accidents.
